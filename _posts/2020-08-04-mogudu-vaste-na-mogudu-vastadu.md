#### Meaning in English:
If the weeping widow's husband returns, mine will come also.
Stolid indifference. Want of feeling.

#### Meaning in Telugu:
మొగుడు వస్తే, నా మొగుడూ వస్తాడు.

#### Romanization:
Moguḍu vastē, nā moguḍū vastāḍu.

Mogudu vaste, na mogudu vastadu.
