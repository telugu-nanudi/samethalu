#### Meaning in English:
If you wish to kill a miser, to ask him for a Dabb is sufficient.
A Dabbu is one-third of an Ana (anna), and is equivalent to a half-penny.

#### Meaning in Telugu:
లోభిని చంపవలెనంటే, డబ్బు అడిగితే చాలును.
