#### Meaning in English:
Don't boil, don't boil, 0 garlick! However much you boil, your smell will not go.
Said to an envious person, who is said to burn inwardly.

#### Meaning in Telugu:
ఉడకకే వుడకకే వో వుల్లిగడ్ద, నీవెంత వుడికినా నీ కంపుపోదు.
