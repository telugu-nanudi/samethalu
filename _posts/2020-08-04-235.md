#### Meaning in English:
His own property is like Ippapindi, his neighbour's like fine molasses.
Ippapindi is the refuse of the nuts of the Ippa tree (Bassia Latifolia) and is remarkably bitter.
Applied to a niggard.

#### Meaning in Telugu:
