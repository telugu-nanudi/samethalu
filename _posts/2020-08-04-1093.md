#### Meaning in English:
When the drum sounded "tudum, tudum" a man called out "durayi, durayi."
Tudum is the (onomatopoetic) name of a kind of drum (Tom-tom ). Durayi is a form of adjuration in the name of Government.
(See No. 40.)
