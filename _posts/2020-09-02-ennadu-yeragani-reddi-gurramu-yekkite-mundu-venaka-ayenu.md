#### Proverb Meaning in English:
The Reddi who had never mounted a horse before, sat with his face to the tail.
(See Nos. 95, 134, 474.)

#### Proverb Meaning in Telugu:
ఎన్నడూ యెరగని రెడ్డి గుర్రము యెక్కితే, ముందూ వెనక ఆయెను.
