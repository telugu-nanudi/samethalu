#### Meaning in English:
While the idol in the temple was in want of Naivedyam, the priest cried for Pulihora.
(See Nos. 227, 989, 1781, 1796, - For Naivedyam see No. 3. )
Pulihora is a more expensive ofl'ering prepared with acid.
