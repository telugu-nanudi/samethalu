#### Meaning in English:
Like ghee poured on fire.
To cast oil in the fire is not the way to quench it.

#### Meaning in Telugu:

#### Romanization:
Agnihōtramulō ajyamu pōśinaṭṭu.
Agnihotramulo ajyamu posinattu.
