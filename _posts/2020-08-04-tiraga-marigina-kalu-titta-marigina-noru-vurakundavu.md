#### Meaning in English:
The feet which are wont to wander, and the mouth which is accustomed to abuse, will never be quiet.

#### Meaning in Telugu:
తిరగ మరిగిన కాలూ తిట్ట మరిగిన నోరూ వూరకుండవు.

#### Romanization:
Tiraga marigina kālū tiṭṭa marigina nōrū vūrakuṇḍavu.
