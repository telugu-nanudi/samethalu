#### Meaning in English:
Poison in the palate, sweetness on the tip of the tongue.
(See Nos. 4, 324, 535.)
Full of courtesy, full of craft.

#### Meaning in Telugu:
అంగిట విషము, మున్నాలికను తియ్యదనము.

#### Romanization:
Aṅgiṭa viṣamu, munnālikanu tiyyadanamu.

Angita visamu, munnalikanu tiyyadanamu.
