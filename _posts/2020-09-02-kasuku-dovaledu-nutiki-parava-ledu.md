#### Proverb Meaning in English:
He has not the means of [getting] one cash, but thinks nothing of [spending] a hundred [pagodas].
(For cash see No. 613.) A pagoda = 3 1/2 Rupees.
Wanton extravagance.
He is able to buy an abbey.
