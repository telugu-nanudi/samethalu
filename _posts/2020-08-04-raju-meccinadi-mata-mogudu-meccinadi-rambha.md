#### Meaning in English:
That's the word, which pleases the king; she is Rambha, who is loved by her husband.

#### Meaning in Telugu:

#### Romanization:
Rāju meccinadi māṭa, moguḍu meccinadi rambha.
Raju meccinadi mata, mogudu meccinadi rambha.
