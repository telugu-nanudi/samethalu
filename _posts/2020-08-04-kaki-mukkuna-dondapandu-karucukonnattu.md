#### Meaning in English:
Like a Donda fruit in a crow's beak.
(See Nos. 7l3, 790,)
Donda is a bright red fruit (Coccinea Indica)
A beautiful thing possessed by a person who is unworthy of it.

#### Meaning in Telugu:
కాకి ముక్కున దొండపండు కరుచుకొన్నట్టు.

#### Romanization:
