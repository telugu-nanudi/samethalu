#### Meaning in English:
Ruined in both ways like a well-digger who turns Sanyasi.
He is neither employed in his old trade nor supported in his new character.

#### Meaning in Telugu:

#### English Transliteration:
ubhaya bhrashTatvaM, vuppara sannyaasitvaM.
