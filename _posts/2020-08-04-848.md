#### Meaning in English:
There is no learning in my house, nor performance of Sandhya in my family.
Said by an uneducated and irreligious Brahman.

#### Meaning in Telugu:
చదువు మా యింట లేదు, సంధ్య మా వంశాన లేదు.

#### English Transliteration:
