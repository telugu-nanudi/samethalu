#### Meaning in English:
If you give, [I shall consider there is] a marriage [in your house] and if you don't give, a funeral.
(See Nos. 243, 1510.)
The impertinent speech of a beggar.

#### Meaning in Telugu:
ఇస్తే పెండ్లి, యివ్వకపోతే పెటాకులు.

#### Romanization:
