#### Meaning in English:
When there's a drought in Makha or Pubba, a great famine will follow.
Makha and Pubba are the 10th and 11th lunar mansions.

#### Meaning in Telugu:
మఖ పుబ్బలు వరపయితే, మహత్తయిన క్షామము.

#### Romanization:
Makha pubbalu varapayitē, mahattayina kṣāmamu.
