#### Proverb Meaning in English:
It is Abbadu who will be ruined, bring me another handful [of money,] I will give it in alms.
(See Nos. 3, 1396, 1560.)
Abbadu is a familiar term applied to a lad.
Hens are ay free o' horse corn. (Scotch)
