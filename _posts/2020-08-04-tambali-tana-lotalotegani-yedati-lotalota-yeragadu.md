#### Meaning in English:
A Tambali prates, but does not listen to what others say.

తంబళి తన లొటలొటేగాని, యెదటి లొటలొట యెరగడు.

#### Romanization:
Tambaḷi tana loṭaloṭēgāni, yedaṭi loṭaloṭa yeragaḍu.
