#### Meaning in English:
A thief's wife is always a widow.
She is always in danger of becoming one.

#### Meaning in Telugu:
దొంగవాడి పెండ్లాము యెప్పుడూ ముండమోపే.

#### Romanization:
Doṅgavāḍi peṇḍlāmu yeppuḍū muṇḍamōpē.
Dongavadi pendlamu yeppudu mundamope.
