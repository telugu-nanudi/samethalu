#### Meaning in English:
If the vital parts escape, there is no comfort like that which follows a wound.
An invalid receives every attention from his friends.

#### Meaning in Telugu:

#### Romanization:
Āyaṁ tappitē gāyamanta sukhamu lēdu.
Ayam tappite gayamanta sukhamu ledu.
