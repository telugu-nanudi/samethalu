#### Meaning in English:
One said " Where's the ladle ?" the other said "Where's the stick ?"
Two lazy fellows.

#### Romanization:
Teḍḍu yēdi aṇṭē, koyya yēdi annaṭṭu.
Teddu yedi ante, koyya yedi annattu.
