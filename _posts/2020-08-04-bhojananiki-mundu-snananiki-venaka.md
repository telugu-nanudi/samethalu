#### Meaning in English:
First to dinner, last to bathe.
The first at dinner gets the best of the lure, the last to bathe gets the best supply of warm water.

#### Meaning in Telugu:
భోజనానికి ముందు, స్నానానికి వెనక.

#### Romanization:
Bhōjanāniki mundu, snānāniki venaka.
