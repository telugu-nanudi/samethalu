#### Meaning in English:
If you feed, [people] will eat; but if you abuse none will bear it.

#### Romanization:
Peṭṭitē tiṇṭārugāni, tiṭṭitē paḍēvāru lēru.

Pettite tintarugani, tittite padevaru leru.
