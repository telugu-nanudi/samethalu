#### Meaning in English:
Like a hawk attacking a flock of cranes.
Svatikonga is a kind of crane which emigrates in Svati, the 15th lunar mansion. Saluva is a hawk used in hunting.

#### Meaning in Telugu:
స్వాతికొంగలమీదికి సాళువ పోయినట్టు.
