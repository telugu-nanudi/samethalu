#### Meaning in English:
The shore is the limit of the sea.
The word Chellelikatta signifies 'sister bank.' The idea is that the shore is as inviolate as a sister.
Used in cautioning others not to transgress due bounds.

#### Meaning in Telugu:

#### Romanization:
