#### Meaning in English:
When addressed [by his son] in the bazar as "Father," he replied, "Son! unto whom wert thou born?"
(See Nos. 412, 1610.)
Want of natural affection. Selfish indifference.

#### Meaning in Telugu:
అంగటివీథిలో అబ్బా అంటే, యెవరికి పుట్టినావురా కొడకా అన్నట్టు.
