#### Meaning in English:
The sight of a beggar rouses a poor man's anger.
(See No. 75.)
Said impertinently by a beggar when he gets nothing.

#### Meaning in Telugu:
బిచ్చపు వాణ్ని చూస్తే బీద వానికి కోపము.

#### Romanization:

Biccapu vanni custe bida vaniki kopamu.
