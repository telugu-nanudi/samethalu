#### Meaning in English:
By the time he had finished equipping himself, the whole town was plundered.
(See no. 2069.)
Procrastination on the part of a boaster to conceal his cowardice or ignorance.

చీర సింగారించేటప్పటికి పట్నమంతా చూరబోయినట్టు.
