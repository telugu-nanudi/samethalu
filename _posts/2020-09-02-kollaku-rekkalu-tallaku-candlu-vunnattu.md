#### Proverb Meaning in English:
Like the wings of fowls, and the flower-stalks of palmyra trees.
(See No. 1063. )
Domestic fowls make no use of their wings, and the flower-stalk (spadix) of the palmyra (See No. 290.) bears no fruit.
Useless possessions.
