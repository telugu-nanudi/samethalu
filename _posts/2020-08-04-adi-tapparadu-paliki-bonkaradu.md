#### Meaning in English:
Having promised, you should not fail; having spoken, you should not lie (retract).

#### Meaning in Telugu:
ఆడి తప్పరాదు, పలికి బొంకరాదు.

#### Romanization:
Āḍi tapparādu, paliki boṅkarādu.
Adi tapparadu, paliki bonkaradu.
