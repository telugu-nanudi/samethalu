#### Meaning in English:
Muttering like a man plucking Kari Vepa leaves.
Kari Vepa is the Bergera Koenigii and is used in making curries. There is an idea that the leaves lose their flavour if plucked silently.

#### Meaning in Telugu:

#### Romanization:
