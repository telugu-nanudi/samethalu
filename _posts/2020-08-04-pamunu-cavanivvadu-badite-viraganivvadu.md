#### Meaning in English:
He won't allow the snake to die, and he won't allow the stick to be broken.
(See No. 46.)
Applied to a trimmer.

పామునూ చావనివ్వడు, బడితే విరగనివ్వడు.

#### Romanization:

Pamunu cavanivvadu, badite viraganivvadu.
