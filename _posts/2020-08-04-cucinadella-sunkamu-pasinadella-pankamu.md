#### Meaning in English:
Every thing that is seen [is subjected to the payment of] duty; and every thing that is rotten [is thrown away in the] mud.
Excessive demands.

#### Meaning in Telugu:
చూచినదెల్లా సుంకము, పాశినదెల్లా పంకము.

#### Romanization:
