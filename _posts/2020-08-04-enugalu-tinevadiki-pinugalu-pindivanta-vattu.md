#### Meaning in English:
To the man that eats elephants, corpses are as pastry.
(See Nos. 694, 779, l008.)
A thoroughly bad man won't stick at trifies.

#### Meaning in Telugu:
ఏనుగలు తినేవాడికి పీనుగలు పిండివంట వత్తు.

#### Romanization:
Ēnugalu tinēvāḍiki pīnugalu piṇḍivaṇṭa vattu.
