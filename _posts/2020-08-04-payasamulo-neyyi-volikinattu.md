#### Meaning in English:
Like the ghi falling into the milk pudding.
(See Nos. 195, 1368, 1430, 1838.)
When a pot of ghi slipped from a woman's hand, it fortunately fell into a pudding.
He is like a cat, he always falls on his feet.
