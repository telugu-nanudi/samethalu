#### Meaning in English:
One said " Loss, loss !" the other replied "A hole in the dish."
A pun on the word Odu.

#### Meaning in Telugu:
ఓడు వోడంటే, కంచం వోడన్నట్టు.

#### Romanization:
Ōḍu vōḍaṇṭē, kan̄caṁ vōḍannaṭṭu.

Odu vodante, kancam vodannattu.
