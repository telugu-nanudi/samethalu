#### Meaning in English:
Better be the hand-maid of a great man than the wife of a low fellow.
Better fare hard with good men than feast with bad.

#### Meaning in Telugu:
అధమునికి ఆలయ్యేటంతకంటే,  బలవంతునికి  బానిసె అయ్యేది మేలు.

#### Romanization:
