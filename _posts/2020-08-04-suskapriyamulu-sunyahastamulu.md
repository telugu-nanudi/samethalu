#### Meaning in English:
Sweet words, empty hands.
(See Nos. 658, 1319, 1711, 1923.)
Speaking kindly, but rendering no assistance.
Deeds are love, and not fine phrases. (Spanish)[1]
Words are female, deeds are male. (Italian)[2]
