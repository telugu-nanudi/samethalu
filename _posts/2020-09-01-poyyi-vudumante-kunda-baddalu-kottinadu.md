#### Proverb Meaning in English:
When asked to blow the fire, he broke the pot which was on it.
To do much harm when asked to do a little favor.

#### Proverb Meaning in Telugu:
పొయ్యి వూదుమంటే, కుండ బద్దలు కొట్టినాడు.

#### Romanization:
