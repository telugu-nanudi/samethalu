#### Meaning in English:
The journey was a sham, but eating (the provisions prepared) was a reality.
(See No. 443.)

#### Meaning in Telugu:
ప్రయాణము అబద్ధము, ప్రసాపాటు నిబద్ధి.

#### Romanization:
Prayāṇamu abad'dhamu, prasāpāṭu nibad'dhi.
Prayanamu abad'dhamu, prasapatu nibad'dhi.
