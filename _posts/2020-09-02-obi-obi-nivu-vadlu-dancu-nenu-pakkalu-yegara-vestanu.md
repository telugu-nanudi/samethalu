#### Proverb Meaning in English:
Obi, Obi, you pound the rice, and I'll shake my sides.
(i.e. imitate the action of pounding).
(See No. 296.)
Applied to an idle skulk, who pretends to be always busy.
Lazy folks take the most pains.
