#### Meaning in English:
The Tonda grew old, and hecarne an Usaravilli.
Tonds is a bloodsucker (chameleon) which when old is called usaravilli.
Vice growing with age.

#### Meaning in Telugu:
తొండ ముదిరి వూసరవిల్లి అయినట్టు.

#### Romanization:

Tonda mudiri vusaravilli ayinattu.
