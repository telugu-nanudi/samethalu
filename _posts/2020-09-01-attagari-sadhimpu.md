#### Proverb Meaning in English:
The rancour of a mother-in-law.
(See Nos. 54, 57, 537.)
Among Hindus the husband's mother, when living, rules the house. and to her the wife is subject.
The husband's mother is the wife's devil. (Germain).[1]
